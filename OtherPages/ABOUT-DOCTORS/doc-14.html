About Dr. Farah Anwar (EMDR Therapist for Adults)

Dr. Farah Anwar is a highly skilled **EMDR Therapist** specializing in trauma recovery and emotional well-being. She has extensive experience in helping adults overcome PTSD, anxiety, and emotional distress through Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapy.

With a deep understanding of trauma-related conditions, Dr. Farah applies evidence-based therapies to support clients in their journey toward healing and resilience. Her compassionate approach ensures that each individual receives personalized care tailored to their unique experiences.

Specialist in Trauma Therapy

Dr. Farah Anwar specializes in **EMDR Therapy** to assist individuals dealing with past traumatic experiences, anxiety, and stress disorders. Her goal is to help clients reprocess traumatic memories in a safe and structured manner.

Holistic Healing & Emotional Support

By integrating EMDR with mindfulness-based techniques, Dr. Farah provides **comprehensive emotional healing** that empowers individuals to regain confidence and mental well-being.

Certified & Accredited Professional

Dr. Farah is a **certified EMDR Therapist** and a member of the **Pakistan Psychological Association (PPA)**, ensuring the highest standards of professional care and ethical practice.
