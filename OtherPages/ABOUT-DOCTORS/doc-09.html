About Dr. Amina Siddiqui (Counsellor & Couples Counsellor at Zara Therapy Clinic)

Dr. Amina Siddiqui is an experienced Counsellor specializing in individual therapy and couples counseling. With a compassionate approach, she helps clients navigate relationship challenges, stress, and emotional well-being.

She integrates Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to support couples and individuals in overcoming conflicts, communication barriers, and anxiety-related issues. Dr. Amina believes in empowering her clients with self-awareness and emotional resilience for long-term harmony.

Relationship & Couples Counselling

Dr. Amina specializes in guiding couples through conflict resolution, emotional connection, and strengthening relationships using Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and Gottman Method Therapy.

Therapeutic Approaches

With expertise in various evidence-based techniques, she offers tailored therapy sessions for individuals and couples, addressing emotional well-being, anxiety, and self-esteem issues.

Registered with Professional Bodies

Dr. Amina Siddiqui is a registered member of the Pakistan Association for Counselling & Psychotherapy (PACP), ensuring professional standards and ethical care.
