About Dr. Saira Iqbal (CBT Therapist for Adults at Zara Therapy Clinic)

Dr. Saira Iqbal is a highly skilled CBT therapist specializing in adult mental health care. She has extensive experience in treating anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders using evidence-based psychological interventions.

With a compassionate and client-centered approach, Dr. Iqbal tailors therapy plans to each individual, ensuring effective, long-term emotional well-being. She integrates cognitive-behavioral techniques to help clients manage negative thoughts and develop healthier coping mechanisms.

Expert in Adult Mental Health

Dr. Saira focuses on providing therapy for adults experiencing anxiety, depression, and work-related stress. She specializes in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to assist individuals in overcoming negative thinking patterns and emotional distress.

Holistic & Evidence-Based Approaches

With a deep understanding of psychological interventions, Dr. Iqbal offers structured therapy sessions designed to create positive behavioral changes and enhance emotional well-being.

Accredited & Recognized Professional

Dr. Saira Iqbal is a registered CBT therapist with the Pakistan Psychological Association (PPA), adhering to the highest ethical and professional standards.
