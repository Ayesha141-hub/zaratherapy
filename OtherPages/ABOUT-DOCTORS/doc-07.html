About Dr. Faisal Raza (Counsellor at Zara Therapy Clinic)

Dr. Faisal Raza is an experienced Counsellor specializing in mental well-being, emotional support, and personal development. He provides a safe space for individuals to explore their thoughts, overcome challenges, and achieve mental clarity.

With years of experience in counseling, Dr. Faisal is skilled in guiding individuals through stress, relationship issues, and self-esteem concerns. His approach integrates mindfulness and solution-focused techniques to support positive mental health.

Experienced Counsellor for Personal Growth

Dr. Faisal specializes in helping individuals navigate life challenges, manage emotions, and build resilience. His sessions are designed to provide practical tools for self-improvement.

Professional Guidance & Support

Using evidence-based counseling techniques, Dr. Faisal tailors his sessions to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring meaningful and lasting progress.

Registered with Professional Bodies

Dr. Faisal Raza is a certified Counsellor, recognized by the Pakistan Psychological Association (PPA), upholding the highest standards of ethical practice.
