About Dr. Umar Farooq (Senior Clinical Psychologist at Zara Therapy Clinic)

Dr. Umar Farooq is a highly experienced Clinical Psychologist with a strong background in mental health care. He specializes in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), providing personalized therapy sessions for individuals dealing with stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges.

With years of expertise in working with diverse populations, Dr. Farooq emphasizes evidence-based techniques to promote emotional well-being, self-awareness, and resilience in his clients.

Child, Adolescent & Adult Psychologist

Dr. Umar specializes in helping individuals navigate mental health challenges through Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and Mindfulness-Based Therapy. His approach fosters emotional stability and long-term recovery.

Expert in Therapy & Counseling

With extensive training in psychotherapy and counseling techniques, Dr. Farooq customizes each session to best support the unique needs of his clients, ensuring effective and meaningful progress.

Certified & Registered Professional

Dr. Umar Farooq is a registered Clinical Psychologist with the Pakistan Psychological Association (PPA), adhering to the highest ethical and professional standards in psychological care.
